Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items with original tags. Items must be in original condition for a full refund.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1-2 days) and international shipping options are also available.

Do you offer size exchanges?

Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Just contact our support team to arrange the exchange.

Are your products sustainably made?

We're committed to sustainable practices and work with suppliers who share our values. Many of our products use organic cotton and recycled materials.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Join our Urban Family program to earn points on purchases, get early access to drops, and receive exclusive member benefits.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your package's progress.
